Highlights

  • A multi-sectoral national emergency operations centre (EOC), activated at Level 2, continues to coordinate the national response activities
  • From 22nd to 28th October 2022, 95 new confirmed cases were recorded in Nigeria
  • To date, 266,138 cases have been confirmed, 259560 cases have been discharged and 3,155 deaths have been recorded in 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory
  • The 95 new cases are reported from 13 States- Lagos (39), FCT (12), Kaduna (12), Taraba (10), Delta (8), Bayelsa (3), Nasarawa (3), Edo (2), Ekiti (2), Oyo (2), Bauchi (1) and Plateau (1)
